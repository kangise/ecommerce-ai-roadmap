> **Tools**: Python + BERTopic + Claude API

## Background

A consumer electronics brand selling Bluetooth earbuds on Amazon US faced the following issues:
- Their product rating dropped from 4.3 to 4.0, with no clear understanding of the specific reasons
- Competitor A's rating rose from 4.1 to 4.4, with no insight into what improvements they made
- Each round of review analysis required operations staff to spend 2 days reading manually

## Traditional Approach (Before)

| Step | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Manually read 200+ negative reviews of own product | 4 hours |
| 2 | Manually categorize issues in Excel | 2 hours |
| 3 | Manually read 200+ positive reviews of Competitor A | 4 hours |
| 4 | Comparative analysis, write report | 3 hours |
| **Total** | | **~13 hours (2 days)** |

Problems:
- Subjective judgment different people produce different analysis results
- Can only review recent reviews, unable to analyze trends
- Cannot quantify the severity of each issue

## AI Pipeline Approach (After)

```python
# Actual code execution flow
pipeline = ReviewAnalysisPipeline()

# Step 1: Load data (5 minutes)
my_reviews = pd.read_csv("my_product_reviews.csv") # 1,200 reviews
comp_reviews = pd.read_csv("competitor_a_reviews.csv") # 980 reviews

# Step 2: Sentiment analysis (3 minutes)
my_results = pipeline.run(my_reviews)
comp_results = pipeline.run(comp_reviews)

# Step 3: Competitive comparison (2 minutes)
comparison = competitive_review_analysis(my_reviews, comp_reviews)

# Step 4: LLM generates insights (5 minutes)
insights = generate_review_insights(my_results, comp_results)
```

## Key Insights Discovered

BERTopic automatically identified 8 negative review topics, ranked by frequency:

| Rank | Topic | Negative Reviews | Share | Keywords |
|------|-------|-----------------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Unstable Bluetooth connection | 47 | 23% | disconnect, bluetooth, connection, drops |
| 2 | Insufficient battery life | 38 | 19% | battery, hours, charge, dies |
| 3 | Uncomfortable fit | 31 | 15% | uncomfortable, ear, pain, falls |
| 4 | Sound quality below expectations | 25 | 12% | sound, bass, quality, tinny |
| 5 | Charging case issues | 18 | 9% | case, charging, lid, broken |

Competitor A's positive review topic analysis revealed:
- Competitor A received numerous positive reviews for "Bluetooth stability" (they likely upgraded their Bluetooth chip)
- Competitor A's "wearing comfort" positive reviews increased (they likely redesigned the ear tips)

## Actionable Recommendations Generated by LLM

```
AI Analysis Report Summary:

1. Most Urgent: Bluetooth connection issues (23% of negative reviews)
→ Recommendation: Upgrade Bluetooth chip to 5.3; Competitor A has already done this
→ Expected impact: 15-20% reduction in negative review rate

2. High Priority: Battery life (19% of negative reviews)
→ Recommendation: Clearly state "actual usage time" rather than "theoretical time" in the Listing
→ Short-term: Adjust Listing to manage expectations; Long-term: Upgrade battery

3. Medium Priority: Wearing comfort (15% of negative reviews)
→ Recommendation: Include different-sized ear tips (S/M/L/XL)
→ Competitor A already offers 4 sizes

4. Listing Optimization Recommendations:
→ Emphasize advantages over Competitor A in Bullet Points
→ Pre-seed Bluetooth connection usage tips in Q&A
```
