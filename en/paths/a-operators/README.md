# Path A: Operators AI-Powered Operations

> Last updated: 2026-03-10

## Overview

- **Target audience**: E-commerce professionals in product research, operations, advertising, and customer service roles
- **Prerequisites**: Basic e-commerce operations experience (familiar with ASINs, PPC, FBA)
- **Time commitment**: 30 minutes per day, 2-4 weeks to complete all modules
- **Key outcome**: A reusable set of AI workflows

> Without writing a single line of code, use AI tools to boost your daily operations efficiency by 3-10x
