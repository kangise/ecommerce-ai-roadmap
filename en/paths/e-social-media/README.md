# Path E: Social Media AI Operations From Traffic to Trust

> Last updated: 2026-03-14

## Overview

Paths A-D focus on e-commerce platform operations themselves. But where does the traffic come from? In 2026, social media is no longer just about "posting for traffic" it's the core battleground for product discovery, brand building, and user trust.

The global social media advertising market is projected to reach $234 billion in 2026 ([SQ Magazine](https://sqmagazine.co.uk/social-media-marketing-statistics/)), with social commerce spending expected to surpass $100 billion. 40% of TikTok users purchase directly within the platform, Instagram Shoppable Reels see 30% higher engagement than regular posts, and Pinterest handles 80 billion monthly searches.

This path helps you systematically leverage AI for social media channel operations, turning "posting" into a replicable, scalable traffic generation system.

> **Recommended prerequisites**: Complete [Path 0 Foundations](../0-foundations/) and the core modules of [Path A Operations](../a-operators/) first. Social media operations are built on product understanding and content capabilities. If you're already running a Shopify DTC store, we recommend also referencing [D1 Shopify Guide](../d-platforms/shopify-ai-guide.md).
