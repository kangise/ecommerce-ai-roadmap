## 2. Otto Marketplace

### 2.1 Otto Core Data

Otto is Germany's second-largest online retailer, with over 12.2 million active buyers, 2.5 million daily visits, and an average of 35 orders per second ([Shoppingfeed](https://blog.shoppingfeed.com/en-us/otto-marketplace-what-brands-need-to-know-about-germanys-second-largest-platform)). The platform uses a curated seller model, accepting only ~5,000+ rigorously vetted sellers to maintain its premium brand image and product quality standards ([Unimall](https://unimall.ai/guides/sell-on-otto)).

| Dimension | Otto | Amazon.de |
|-----------|------|-----------|
| Seller Count | ~5,000+ (curated) | Hundreds of thousands |
| Active Buyers | 12.2M | More |
| Quality Positioning | Premium / curated | All categories |
| Return Rate | High (fashion >50%) | Medium |
| Brand Control | Strong (strict vetting) | Medium |
| Ad System | Basic | Mature (PPC) |

### 2.2 Onboarding Requirements (2026 Update)

In 2026, Otto is opening to all EU sellers, removing previous restrictions on non-German sellers regarding payment and VAT ([Marketplace Universe](https://marketplace-universe.com/entering-germany-beyond-amazon-why-brands-should-look-otto/)).

Per Otto Market official requirements ([otto.market](https://www.otto.market/en/faq/faq.html)):

| Requirement | Description | Mandatory |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|
| Legal Entity | German or Dutch company legal form | Yes |
| VAT ID | German or Dutch VAT number | Yes |
| German Customer Service | Must provide German-language customer service | Yes |
| EU Warehouse Shipping | Ship from German or EU warehouse | Yes |
| Return Acceptance | Accept returns in Germany or designated EU countries | Yes |
| EPR Compliance | Extended Producer Responsibility registration | Yes (non-compliance = immediate sales suspension) |
| VerpackG | German Packaging Act registration | Yes |
| WEEE | Electronic waste recycling registration | Yes (electronics mandatory) |

Content rephrased for compliance with licensing restrictions.

> **Cross-Border Seller Note**: Chinese sellers currently cannot directly onboard to Otto — they need a German/Dutch legal entity or agency. This is Otto's biggest difference from Amazon — higher threshold but less competition.

## 5. European Compliance Requirements (Detailed)

> 📎 **Related Reading**: [A6 Compliance & Risk Management](../a-operators/a6-compliance.md#a6-compliance-risk-management) — Detailed multi-market compliance methodology from A6; CE certification, EPR, VAT, and other general compliance frameworks can be directly reused.

This is the biggest barrier to entering the European market — must be completed before onboarding:

| Compliance Item | Description | Est. Cost | Time | Mandatory |
|----------------|-------------|-----------|------|-----------|
| CE Mark | EU product safety certification | $500-5,000 (varies by category) | 4-12 weeks | Yes |
| GDPR | Data protection (if collecting user data) | Legal consultation fees | Ongoing | Yes |
| EPR | Extended Producer Responsibility (packaging/electronics/batteries) | €200-500/year/country | 2-4 weeks | Yes |
| VerpackG | German Packaging Act | €50-200/year | 1-2 weeks | Germany mandatory |
| WEEE | Electronic waste recycling registration | €200-500/year | 2-4 weeks | Electronics mandatory |
| VAT | Value Added Tax registration | €500-1,000 (registration) + ongoing filing | 4-8 weeks | Yes |
| LUCID | German packaging registration number | Included in VerpackG | 1 week | Germany mandatory |
| Battery Regulation | Battery regulation (2024 new rule) | Depends on battery type | 4-8 weeks | Battery products mandatory |
| GPSR | General Product Safety Regulation (2024 new rule) | Requires EU Authorized Representative | Ongoing | Yes |

### 5.1 EU Authorized Representative

Starting 2024, all non-EU sellers must designate an EU Authorized Representative:
- Representative is responsible for maintaining product compliance documentation
- Representative information must be on product labels
- Cost: €500-2,000/year (depending on service provider)

### 5.2 VAT Registration and Filing

| Country | VAT Rate | Registration Threshold | Filing Frequency |
|---------|----------|----------------------|------------------|
| Germany | 19% | No threshold (must register) | Monthly/Quarterly |
| France | 20% | No threshold | Monthly/Quarterly |
| Italy | 22% | No threshold | Monthly/Quarterly |
| Spain | 21% | No threshold | Quarterly |
| Netherlands | 21% | No threshold | Quarterly |

> **OSS (One-Stop Shop)**: Since 2021, the EU offers OSS to simplify VAT filing. Register OSS in one country and file VAT for all EU countries through a single return.

