## 1. AliExpress Current Status & Positioning

### 1.1 AliExpress vs Temu

> 📎 **Related Reading**: [D5 Temu Seller Strategy](d5-temu-seller-guide.md#semi-managed-vs-fully-managed-profit-comparison-model) — Detailed Temu analysis in D5, including fully managed/semi-managed model comparison and onboarding decision framework.

| Dimension | AliExpress | Temu |
|-----------|-----------|------|
| Model | Seller self-operated | Platform controls pricing and traffic |
| Pricing Control | Seller sets prices | Platform sets prices |
| Brand Space | Yes (brand flagship stores) | Almost none |
| Logistics | Seller chooses (Cainiao/self-ship) | Platform unified |
| Profit Margin | Medium | Extremely low |
| Growth Trend | Slowing | Explosive growth |

### 1.2 AliExpress Differentiated Advantages

- Fully managed mode (AliExpress Choice): Similar to Temu but sellers have more control
- Brand flagship stores: Suitable for branded sellers
- Strong in Southern Europe: High market share in Spain, France, Portugal
- Alibaba ecosystem: Integrated with 1688, Cainiao logistics

## 2. AliExpress Fee Structure & Onboarding

AliExpress currently accepts sellers from: mainland China, Russia, Spain, Italy, Turkey, France, Brazil, etc. ([Wise](https://wise.com/us/blog/sell-on-aliexpress)). Onboarding requires business license, legal representative ID, tax information, etc.

## 3. AI Application Scenarios

### 3.1 AliExpress Choice (Fully Managed Mode) Deep Dive

AliExpress Choice is AliExpress's answer to Temu's fully managed model:

| Dimension | AliExpress Choice | Temu Fully Managed | AliExpress Regular |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Pricing Control | Platform suggests price, seller can fine-tune | Platform fully controls | Seller autonomous |
| Logistics | Platform unified (5-10 days) | Platform unified (7-15 days) | Seller chooses |
| Traffic | Choice label boost | Platform allocated | Organic + ads |
| Returns | Platform handles | Platform handles | Seller handles |
| Best For | Sellers with some brand presence | Pure supply chain sellers | Sellers with strong operational skills |

### 3.2 Multi-Language Listing Optimization

> 📎 **Related Reading**: [A2 Listing Optimization](../a-operators/a2-listing-optimization.md#1-listing-methodology-the-fundamentals-before-ai) — Multi-language localization methodology from A2; Listing optimization framework can be adapted for AliExpress multi-language versions.

AliExpress covers the globe — multi-language is a core need. AliExpress ranks among the top in Southern European markets (Spain, France, Portugal) ([Marketplace Universe](https://marketplace-universe.com/aliexpress-europe/)), making these priority optimization targets.

```
你是一个 AliExpress 多语言 Listing 优化专家。

产品：[名称]
品类：[X]
主要目标市场：[西班牙/法国/俄罗斯/巴西]

请生成以下语言版本的 Listing：

1. 西班牙语（西班牙市场，AliExpress 在西班牙排名前 3）
2. 法语（法国市场）
3. 俄语（俄罗斯/CIS 市场）
4. 巴西葡语（巴西市场）

每个版本包含：
- 标题（AliExpress 标题格式：品牌+产品+核心属性+关键词，≤128 字符）
- 描述（结构化，包含使用场景和规格）
- 5 个卖点
- 10 个当地语言搜索关键词

注意 AliExpress 特殊性：
- 标题可以比 Amazon 更长（128 字符）
- 描述支持 HTML 格式
- 南欧市场（西班牙/法国/葡萄牙）是 AliExpress 最强市场
- 俄罗斯市场虽然受制裁影响但仍有需求
```

### 3.4 AliExpress 2026 Changes

Based on external reporting ([ad-hoc-news.de](https://www.ad-hoc-news.de/news/ueberblick/aliexpress-in-2026-is-the-ultra-cheap-marketplace-still-worth-it-for-us/68641516)), AliExpress has undergone important changes in 2025-2026:

- Tightened buyer protection rules (stricter refund and dispute handling)
- Improved US market logistics (shorter delivery times)
- Strengthened counterfeit and IP infringement crackdowns (under USTR review pressure)
- TikTok and YouTube-driven traffic growth (social media seeding → AliExpress purchase)

> **Real Case: TikTok Hauls Driving AliExpress Growth**
> In 2026, AliExpress became a hot topic again, mainly due to TikTok unboxing videos (hauls), YouTube Shorts, and US resellers flipping AliExpress products on Etsy/Amazon/Depop ([Ad-Hoc News](https://www.ad-hoc-news.de/boerse/news/ueberblick/aliexpress-in-2026-cheap-dupe-paradise-or-shipping-nightmare-for-u-s/68623881)). However, US customs rules, state taxes, and shipping costs are tightening, so sellers need to pay more attention to compliance.
