# Path C: Managers AI Strategy Implementation

> Last updated: 2026-03-11

## Overview

- **Target audience**: Team leads / Founders
- **Prerequisites**: No technical background needed, but a deep understanding of the business is required
- **Time commitment**: 3-5 focused hours to complete assessment and planning
- **Key outcome**: An AI implementation roadmap

> Understand what AI can do for your team and create an actionable AI implementation plan
